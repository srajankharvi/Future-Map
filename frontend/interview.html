Interview Tips & Best Practices

Before the Interview

  • Research the organization thoroughly
  • Review job description carefully
  • Practice common questions
  • Prepare examples (STAR method)
  • Plan your route and timing

During the Interview

  • Arrive 10-15 minutes early
  • Maintain eye contact and smile
  • Listen carefully before answering
  • Give concise, relevant answers
  • Ask thoughtful questions

After the Interview

  • Send a thank you email
  • Reiterate interest in the role
  • Mention key discussion points
  • Keep them informed of availability
  • Follow up if no response in 1-2 weeks
